01About

At Welspun, I build the systems yard operations run on: a digital twin rendered from live SAP data, executive dashboards for plant leadership, and rule engines that surface issues spreadsheets miss.

02Systems in Production
    / 01Live+

    Yard Digital Twin

    A geo-accurate 3D model of the storage yards, rendered live from SAP stock data. Dual 3D/2D renderers, a card-based ZWASR13 import pipeline, and production & dispatch trends on a shared timeline.

    Three.jsWebGLSAPJavaScript

    Built under a strict additive-only engineering discipline with a validation gate on every release. The SAP import stages on drop and runs on an explicit command, so the model never drifts from source data.

    / 02+

    AI Intelligence Layer

    A deterministic state machine and rule engine that flags illegal pipe-state transitions, with an LLM layered on top as explainer and diagnostician. Detection in code, diagnosis in AI.

    State machinesRule engineLLM

    The rule engine owns detection, so results stay deterministic and auditable; the model only explains why a flagged transition is illegal and what to do about it.

    / 03+

    Executive KPI Dashboards

    Daily operations scorecards for plant leadership — targets, RAG status, and week-on-week deltas — across two plants, on a consistent reporting standard.

    RechartsTableauBISAP

    Designed to a repeatable house style: daily scorecards with targets and RAG status, week-on-week delta blocks, and remarks — readable in ten seconds by leadership.

    / 04+

    SAP Inventory Analytics

    Reconciliation and analysis of SAP warehouse exports that surfaced unlocated tonnage, default-bin dumping, and negative-stock artifacts — and turned them into the case for digital infrastructure.

    PythonSAP WM/MMReconciliation

    Every figure was reconciled against source export totals before any conclusion — turning WE-ZONE and default-bin dumping from data noise into a quantified case for investment.

    / 05+

    Fleet Telematics Attribution

    Driver-to-vehicle attribution across a 34-asset fleet, bridging Motive telematics into timekeeping so utilization and cost data are accurate.

    MotiveKronosData engineering

    Untangled the "Unidentified Driver" problem so utilization and cost finally tie back to real people and shifts — the prerequisite for any trustworthy cost dashboard.

    / 06Independent+

    Latur Economic Initiative

    An independent feasibility study modeling three processing & storage ventures from a 226-page district plan, paired with an interactive ROI dashboard.

    RechartsFinancial modeling

    Found that manufacturing carried the highest growth elasticity yet was misclassified as "maintain", then modeled three ventures with ROI, capital, and risk side by side.
